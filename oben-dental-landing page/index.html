Our Services

What We Provide

  • Prosthesis e.g. Dentures

    A dental prosthesis is detachable equipment used to replace lost teeth. Dentures, bridges, and partial dentures are all examples of dental prostheses. These appliances are usually composed of acrylic, metal, or ceramic and are meant to fit over the remaining teeth or gums to replace lost teeth.

  • Periodontics

    We treat complex periodontal cases with a mixture of non-surgical and surgical treatments, tailored to each individual patientâ€™s needs. Treatments include removal of the bacteria that cause the condition, education about how to manage the condition and stop it from progressing, and in some cases procedures to repair damaged bone and to re-contour the gums.

  • Cosmetic Dentistry

    Cosmetic dentistry has to do with everything that would improve your appearance Cosmetic dentistry primarily focuses on improvement in dental aesthetics in color, position, shape, size, alignment and overall smile appearance.

  • Invisalign Full Treatment

    Invisalign is a removable brace system that uses a series of custom-moulded aligners to move your teeth. The great thing about Invisalign is that the aligners are transparent and invisible (no metal bands or wires).

  • Live Advisory

    This service provides information on public services and entitlements in Oben Dentistry. Our Live Advisory allows you to chat directly with an Information Officer in a chat-room-type environment. The Live Advisory service is intended for people with hearing and speech difficulties and for others who have difficulty communicating by telephone. The Live Advisor service is available from 9am to 5pm, Monday to Friday.

  • Dental Implant

    With dental implants, you can wear your smiles ideally as there are no clues of artificial restoration if constructed by a skilled dentist.
